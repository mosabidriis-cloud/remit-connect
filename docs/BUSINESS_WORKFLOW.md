# REOS Business Workflow

## Purpose

This document defines the operational workflows followed by Remit Exchange and serves as the business reference for REOS development.

## Core Operational Principles

- Business workflow drives software design.
- One operational process at a time.
- Minimize manual errors.
- Maximize operational visibility.
- Maintain auditability.

## High-Level Operational Flow

1. Shared Batch Received
2. Batch Assignment
3. Credit to Account Workspace
4. Sequential Transaction Processing
5. Proof of Payment
6. Batch Completion
7. Treasury Support (when required)
8. Operational Reporting

## Operational Modules

### Operations Control Center

Purpose:
Real-time operational monitoring.

### Shared Batches

Purpose:
Receive and assign batches.

### Credit to Account

Purpose:
Process assigned batches.

### Transaction Processing

Purpose:
Guide officers through safe sequential processing.

### Treasury Support

Purpose:
Manage branch funding requirements.

### Reporting

Purpose:
Operational monitoring and performance analysis.

## Guiding Principle

REOS must always reflect the real Remit Exchange operation. Business workflow changes must be documented here before implementation.
